Entailed is small on purpose. It is not trying to be an endless open world. It is a focused game about noticing things, moving through charming spaces, and solving practical physical puzzles that feel like they belong in the environment.

A tire swing is not just scenery. A rain barrel is not just decoration. A suspicious wall is probably suspicious for a reason.

Entailed

A cozy low-poly puzzle adventure about tiny enclosed worlds, hidden objects, and playful physical problem-solving.

Explore compact walled-yard spaces filled with sheds, gardens, wells, ponds, old tools, strange corners, and objects that feel just a little too useful to ignore. Each space is designed like a small papercraft diorama: easy to understand at a glance, but full of details worth poking at.

Targeting a short-term Papercraft Games release window.

  • Small enclosed levels with one clear goal, a few secrets, and one main tactile puzzle.
  • Push, roll, swing, redirect, float, lift, lower, tilt, and nudge useful objects into place.
  • A focused adventure about noticing details and getting satisfying little aha moments.

Concept art, not final game art

All game art shown on this page is concept art and should not be read as final game art. Some concept images are exploratory AI-generated images. Final game art will be human-directed and production-made; if AI is used later, it will be a small, clearly handled part of the overall art process.

Open-source toolchain

Folded Paper Engine

Folded Paper Engine is Papercraft Games' Blender-to-Godot workflow for creating stylized game spaces and interactive mechanics. It lets us tag gameplay in Blender, export standard GLTF, and open playable Godot scenes with players, triggers, physics, audio, inventory items, scene metadata, and animation events.
